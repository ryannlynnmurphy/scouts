A. Pixel Art

Blocky, retro, expressive. Multiple emotion sprites per character. Think Coffee Talk, VA-11 Hall-A. Easy to create, strong aesthetic identity. Works great with the dark forest palette.

QUINN
BRENT

B. Illustrated / Drawn

Clean lines, large expressive eyes, anime-adjacent. Multiple expressions per character. Think Ace Attorney, Doki Doki. Higher fidelity, more emotional range. Needs more art work but reads as "real game."

QUINN
BRENT

C. Silhouettes with Color

No faces, no features -- just shape and color. Theatrical, abstract, haunting. Each character is their color. Brent is a bigger, redder shape. Quinn is smaller, softer lavender. The anonymity IS the point -- they're being stripped of identity. Cheapest to build, strongest artistic statement.

QUINN
BRENT

D. Stylized / Posterized

AI-generated base portraits run through heavy posterize/threshold filters. 3-4 colors per character. Scan lines overlay. A Scanner Darkly meets rotoscope. Unsettling, dreamlike. Medium effort -- generate then process.

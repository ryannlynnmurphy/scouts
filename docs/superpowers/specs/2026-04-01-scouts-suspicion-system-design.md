# SCOUTS -- Suspicion System & Branching Paths Design

**Core Mechanic Revision:** The game is played from Simon's perspective. Sam is his lover. They are ALREADY together when the game begins -- they came to boy scout camp trying to hide it. Every interaction with Sam is a risk. The goal: survive Brent's ritual without him discovering that you and Sam are together. If he does, things get worse.

---

## The Two Hidden Meters

### 1. Fracture (existing -- unchanged)
How broken Simon is by performing toxic masculinity. Drives visual/audio degradation.

### 2. Suspicion (NEW)
How much Brent suspects Simon and Sam are together. 0.0 to 1.0.

**What raises suspicion:**
- Whispering to Sam during Brent scenes
- Looking at Sam (choosing "look at Sam" in reaction moments)
- Backing Sam up when Brent attacks him
- Reacting too strongly when Sam is threatened
- Getting caught in Gay Shit scenes (if suspicion is already high, Brent notices they're gone)
- The kiss test -- if Simon shows ANY reaction during the kiss with Sam
- Simon's pickup line -- if it sounds too soft/loving (echoing how he talks to Sam)

**What lowers suspicion:**
- Performing well (saying the right toxic things)
- Distancing from Sam visibly (choosing to NOT sit near him, NOT whisper)
- Laughing at Sam when Brent mocks him (devastating to do but effective)
- Directing Brent's attention elsewhere ("Hey what about Josh?")

**Suspicion is NOT shown to the player.** They feel it through Brent's behavior:
- **Low suspicion (0-0.3):** Brent treats Simon like any other boy. Generic "faggot" directed at the group.
- **Medium suspicion (0.3-0.6):** Brent starts singling Simon out. Extra questions. "You two seem REAL close." More "faggot" directed specifically at Simon. He separates Simon and Sam during group activities.
- **High suspicion (0.6-0.8):** Brent is actively testing Simon. He forces Simon and Sam apart. He makes pointed comments. "Hey Sam, why do you keep looking at Simon?" The other boys get uncomfortable.
- **Critical suspicion (0.8+):** Brent KNOWS. The ritual changes. See "The Exposure Path" below.

## The Tension Triangle

Every player choice now exists on THREE axes:

```
        AUTHENTIC
           /\
          /  \
         /    \
        /      \
       /        \
      /  CHOICE  \
     /            \
    /______________\
PERFORMED      PROTECTIVE
(of Sam)
```

- **AUTHENTIC:** True to yourself. Risks fracture AND suspicion.
- **PERFORMED:** Comply with Brent. Lowers suspicion but raises fracture.
- **PROTECTIVE:** Shield Sam specifically. May raise suspicion but keeps Sam safe. Different from authentic -- you're not being yourself, you're sacrificing yourself for him.

Some choices add a fourth option:
- **DEFLECT TO ANOTHER BOY:** Redirect Brent's attention to Josh, Noah, or Lucas. Lowers suspicion on Simon/Sam but throws someone else under the bus. Raises fracture (you feel guilty).

## How Suspicion Changes Existing Scenes

### Scene 1: The Oath + Sam's Interrogation

**Current:** Brent interrogates Simon, then Sam recites the outdoor code.

**NEW with suspicion:**

When Sam finishes the outdoor code and Brent starts mocking him:

CHOICE (12s timer):
- Whisper to Sam: "You were right." [PROTECTIVE +0.04 suspicion, +0.03 fracture]
- Look at Sam with concern [PROTECTIVE +0.02 suspicion, +0.01 fracture] -- subtler, but Brent might catch the look
- Laugh along with Brent [PERFORMED -0.02 suspicion, +0.05 fracture] -- Sam hears you laugh at him
- Stay quiet, look at the ground [DEFLECT +0.00 suspicion, +0.01 fracture]

**NEW CONSEQUENCE:** If player chose to whisper or look at Sam:
Brent adds a line (not in original script): "Simon, you got something to say to your BOYFRIEND over there?"
Simon: "What? No."
Brent: "Then keep your mouth shut."
Suspicion +0.05 (he noticed)

### Scene 1: The Squirrel Sacrifice

**Current:** Simon has to kill the squirrel.

**NEW with suspicion:**

When everyone has killed their squirrel except Simon, Sam looks at Simon. The player gets an INTERNAL choice:

CHOICE (no timer, internal):
- Meet Sam's eyes [+0.02 suspicion -- Brent might see the look between them]
- Look away from Sam [+0.01 fracture -- you're shutting him out]
- Look at Brent [+0.00 suspicion -- performing attention to the alpha]

**If suspicion > 0.3 at this point,** Brent adds:
"SIMON AND SAM, STOP MAKING EYES AT EACH OTHER AND KILL YOUR GODDAMN SQUIRRELS."
The other boys laugh nervously. Suspicion +0.05.

### Scene 1: The Kiss Test

**This is the CRITICAL suspicion scene.** The script has them paired: Sam and Simon kiss.

Before the kiss, NEW CHOICE:
CHOICE (8s timer):
- Try to get paired with someone else: "Can I kiss Josh instead?" [PROTECTIVE -0.05 suspicion but +0.04 fracture -- you're pushing Sam away to protect both of you]
- Accept the pairing silently [+0.00, proceed to kiss]
- "Sure!" (Simon's script line -- eager) [+0.03 suspicion -- too enthusiastic]

During the kiss:
CHOICE (no timer, time slows):
- Kiss Sam and FEEL it. Eyes close. Linger. [+0.06 suspicion, -0.03 fracture -- authentic but DANGEROUS]
- Kiss Sam mechanically. Quick. Pull away fast. [+0.01 suspicion, +0.03 fracture -- performing]
- Don't kiss. Turn your head at the last moment. [+0.02 suspicion (suspicious avoidance), +0.02 fracture]

After the kiss -- "Check for boners":
CHOICE (5s timer):
- "Mosquito bite." (join the lie) [PERFORMED, -0.01 suspicion]
- Stay silent [+0.01 suspicion]
- "I didn't feel anything." (too emphatic denial) [+0.02 suspicion -- protesting too much]

**If suspicion > 0.5 after the kiss scene,** Brent adds:
"Simon. Sam. You two took a REALLY long time with that kiss."
"Looked pretty comfortable to me."
The other boys go quiet. This is a new danger zone.

### Scene 1: Pickup Lines

**If suspicion > 0.4,** Brent SPECIFICALLY forces Sam to go right before Simon:

Sam's line: "You're my girl... and I like your ass."
Brent: "Little and perky, Sam? Is that what you like? Sounds like you're describing SIMON over there."

NEW CHOICE for Simon (5s timer):
- "Shut up, Brent." [PROTECTIVE +0.06 suspicion -- you just confirmed it]
- Laugh it off: "Good one, Brent." [PERFORMED -0.02 suspicion, +0.04 fracture]
- Say nothing. Go red. [+0.03 suspicion -- your silence says everything]

Then Simon's turn:
**If suspicion is high,** Brent gives an additional instruction: "And Simon? Make sure it sounds like you're talking to a WOMAN. Not a BOY."

### The Resistance Scene

**If suspicion > 0.5,** the resistance plays differently.

When the boys push back against Brent, Brent uses Simon and Sam as ammunition:
"Oh you wanna talk about what's WRONG? Let's talk about SIMON AND SAM. Anyone else notice how these two are ALWAYS together? Always whispering? Always looking at each other?"

NEW CHOICE (no timer -- this is a crisis):
- "We're friends. That's it." [PERFORMED -0.05 suspicion, +0.06 fracture -- you just denied Sam]
- "So what if we are?" [AUTHENTIC +0.15 suspicion (explosion), -0.05 fracture -- you just came out in front of everyone]
- Let Sam answer [DEFLECT +0.03 suspicion] -- Sam says: "We're scouts. We look out for each other."
- Turn it on Brent: "Why are YOU so obsessed with us, Brent?" [+0.04 suspicion but rattles Brent]

## The Gay Shit Scenes (Suspicion Context)

The Gay Shit scenes are when Simon and Sam sneak away from the group. In the play, there's a theatrical transition. In the game, the transition depends on suspicion:

**Low suspicion (< 0.3):** Clean transition. Fade to blue. The cliff/meadow/lake. No tension about being caught. The full Gay Shit experience plays out peacefully.

**Medium suspicion (0.3-0.6):** Before each Gay Shit scene, a new beat:
Sam whispers: "Come on, let's get out of here."
CHOICE (10s timer):
- Follow Sam [Proceed to Gay Shit scene, but suspicion +0.03 when you return]
- "We can't. He's watching." [Skip the Gay Shit scene entirely. Fracture +0.08 from denial. This is DEVASTATING -- you lose the healing.]
- "Give me a second." [Proceed, but shorter scene -- some dialogue is cut, you're nervous the whole time]

**High suspicion (0.6+):** Before Gay Shit Act 3 (the lake), Brent FOLLOWS them.
NEW SCENE: Simon and Sam are at the lake. The "I spy" game starts. Then:
A branch snaps. Brent is there. He's been watching.
Brent: "Well well well."
This triggers THE EXPOSURE PATH (see below).

## The Five Paths the Ritual Can Take

### Path 1: THE INVISIBLE PATH (Suspicion < 0.3 at end)

Simon performed perfectly. Brent never suspected. The ritual plays out like the script. Maximum fracture because Simon suppressed everything. The Gay Shit scenes are the only release.

**Ending:** Simon walks out of the woods. No one knows who he really is. Not even Sam knows how much Simon sacrificed to keep them safe. The fracture is high. The flower never fully bloomed because Simon was too scared to open up even in the safe spaces.

**Final text:** "Simon walked out of the woods the way he walked in. Invisible. Safe. Empty."

### Path 2: THE SUSPICION PATH (Suspicion 0.3-0.6 at ceremony)

Brent has been circling. He suspects but can't confirm. The ceremony is MORE targeted -- Brent directs extra attention to Simon. The alpha chants have Brent standing in Simon's face. The "one thing you can change" becomes pointed:

Brent (to Simon specifically): "What about YOU? What are you gonna change?"
CHOICE:
- "I won't ever feel anything ever again." [Script faithful, PERFORMED]
- "I won't be a faggot." [PERFORMED -- the most devastating option. Saying it in front of Sam.]
- "I won't hide anymore." [AUTHENTIC -- suspicion explodes, triggers Exposure]

**Ending varies** by fracture and whether Simon held it together.

### Path 3: THE DEFIANCE PATH (Simon chose "So what if we are?" or similar)

Simon outed himself and Sam. The group splits. Some boys are disgusted, some are supportive:
- Josh sides with Brent ("I KNEW they were fags")
- Lucas defends Simon ("Who cares?")
- Noah is conflicted (logic vs social pressure)

The ceremony becomes a TRIAL. Brent forces Simon to prove he's "not a fag" through escalating tests. The fight happens because Brent ATTACKS Simon specifically, not as a general "slap each other" -- as punishment.

**Ending:** "Simon walked out of the woods with blood on his hands and Sam's hand in his. He didn't know if it was worth it. But he knew who he was."

### Path 4: THE EXPOSURE PATH (Brent catches them at the lake)

Brent follows Simon and Sam to Gay Shit Act 3. He watches the I Spy game. He sees the kiss.

NEW SCENE -- THE INTERRUPTION:
Brent steps out of the trees.
"What the FUCK did I just see?"
Sam: "Brent, it's not--"
Brent: "DON'T. I SAW IT."

CHOICE (5s timer):
- "So what?" [DEFIANT -- triggers the defiance path from here]
- "It was a joke. Like earlier." [PERFORMED -- try to use the "mosquito bite" logic against him]
- Step in front of Sam [PROTECTIVE -- literally shield him with your body]
- Run [DEFLECT -- scatter into the woods]

If "It was a joke":
Brent: "A JOKE? You were about to KISS HIM."
Simon: "You made us kiss earlier! You said it proves we're NOT--"
Brent's logic collapses. He can't argue against his own test. 
But he's FURIOUS. The ceremony becomes punishment.

If stepped in front of Sam:
Brent grabs Simon. Sam tries to intervene.
Brent: "STAY OUT OF THIS, SAM. Unless you want to get hurt too."
The scene becomes about whether Simon can protect Sam or if the violence turns on both of them.

**Ending:** "The stars were still there. Even after everything. Simon looked up and they were still there."

### Path 5: THE PROTECTION PATH (Simon consistently chose to protect Sam over self)

Throughout the game, Simon took every hit aimed at Sam. Laughed when Brent mocked Sam (to deflect attention). Performed extra hard when Sam was in danger. Said "mosquito bite" immediately. Distanced in public, held on in private.

The Gay Shit scenes are INTENSE -- Simon finally drops the mask and it all comes flooding out. The fracture is high but the love is fierce. Sam asks "Are you okay?" and Simon breaks.

The fight happens as scripted, but Simon's motivation is clear: he's not angry at Brent for what Brent did to HIM. He's angry for what Brent ALMOST did to Sam.

**Ending:** "Simon walked out of the woods alone. He told Sam to go ahead. He needed a minute. He sat on a stump and looked at his hands. Then he looked at the flower Sam gave him. It was still blooming."

## Suspicion-Responsive Dialogue (Brent's Ad-Libs)

Throughout the game, Brent gets additional lines based on suspicion level. These are INSERTED into the existing script at natural pauses:

**Suspicion 0.2+:**
- "Simon, stop looking at Sam."
- "Why are you two always whispering?"
- (to the group) "Anyone else think these two are a little TOO close?"

**Suspicion 0.4+:**
- "SAM. Move away from Simon. NOW."
- "You know what, Simon? I'm gonna put you at the OTHER end of the line."
- "Simon, your pickup line sounded a lot like you were talking to SAM."
- (separating them physically -- Sam's sprite moves to the opposite side)

**Suspicion 0.6+:**
- "I've got my eye on you two."
- "Something's going on and I WILL find out."
- "Simon. Sam. Front and center. Separate. NOW."
- During the ceremony: "This next part is SPECIFICALLY for Simon and Sam."

**Suspicion 0.8+:**
- "EVERYONE STOP. I need to address something."
- Triggers the Exposure confrontation.

## Implementation Notes

### Suspicion Manager (new system)
- `game/systems/SuspicionManager.ts`
- Similar structure to FractureManager
- No visual effects -- suspicion is felt through DIALOGUE, not visuals
- Tracks: suspicion level, which specific moments triggered suspicion, whether exposure has happened
- Exposes: `isSuspicious()`, `isExposed()`, `getBrentAdLib()` (returns additional Brent lines to insert)

### Dialogue Manager Updates
- Before displaying each beat, check `SuspicionManager.getBrentAdLib()` for any suspicion-responsive lines to INSERT
- Ad-lib lines appear as additional DialogueLine objects injected before or after the current beat's lines
- Ad-libs have speaker "brent" and are marked with a flag so they can be styled slightly differently (maybe Brent's portrait is more intense)

### Scene Script Updates
- Each existing beat gets a `suspicionChoices` field alongside `choices` -- additional choices that only appear at certain suspicion thresholds
- New beats for exposure/interruption scenes
- The Gay Shit scene entry points get suspicion gates (the "we can't, he's watching" pre-beat)

### Character Sprite Updates
- At medium+ suspicion, Brent's sprite physically moves CLOSER to Simon and Sam
- At high suspicion, Brent's idle animation changes -- he's staring at Simon instead of pacing
- Sam's sprite subtly shifts away from Simon when suspicion is high (he senses the danger)

## The Emotional Core

The play is about the performance of toxic masculinity crushing queerness.

The game adds: **the specific terror of hiding your love to survive.**

Every choice with Sam is now weighted with fear. "I want to look at him. I want to touch him. I want to tell him it's okay. But if I do, Brent will see. And if Brent sees, we're both dead."

That's the game. That's what makes it devastating.

The flower that never loses its color? That's Sam's love surviving everything the system throws at it. The flower grows when Simon is brave enough to be himself WITH Sam, even when it's dangerous. The flower stays a bud when Simon is too scared to reach for it.

And if Brent catches them at the lake, and Simon steps in front of Sam -- the flower blooms immediately. Full. Bright. In front of everyone. Because love isn't something you hide. Even when it costs you everything.
